About Oldham Business Edge

OLDHAM Business Edge was launched in 2015 following calls from local businesses and professionals for a new platform to promote business news in Oldham and beyond. Overall, we forecast plenty of opportunities to highlight business successes, discuss the issues of the day and meet a broad spectrum of commerce and industry. Employers are keen to highlight the good careers, workplaces, leisure and lifestyle opportunities offered in the borough. Oldham is currently enjoying significant investment and redevelopment. It is also well-located between Manchester and West Yorkshire and includes part of the beautiful Peak District National Park.

We feature a range of businesses demonstrating innovation and excellence in their sectors from technology, science, engineering and manufacturing to property, housing, education, leisure and sport. Some of these trade regionally while others focus on national and international contracts. Working with the Oldham Evening Chronicle, a number of companies have backed the magazine and associated projects with financial support by becoming partners.
